I've had some mess-ups here and there. Some of them were messy, so if you don't want to be cleaning a whole lotta butter, read these tips.

  1. Be careful when you open the bag of flour
  2. Don't put the butter in the microwave for a long time. Put it in for a few seconds at a time(30 sec), otherwise it will burst everywhere.
  3. Mix carefully, it might spill.
  4. Be careful with the blender.
  5. Melt and put in the butter last or mix it in right after you pour it. Once, I put it in and did not mix it, and it hardened, so I couldn't mix it.(If it does happen, try to put the bowl on the stovetop(if you can!)for a few seconds.)
  6. Don't put metal stuff in the microwave! It's very dangerous. No metal spoons, forks, bowls, knives, etc.
  7. Careful with knives when chopping up stuff.
  8. Add your own spin to recipes. Bananas on top of the banana bread, maybe some walnuts. Sugar on the cupcakes. Something like that.
  9. Always double check the temprature and the amount of time it's supposed to be in before putting the food in.
